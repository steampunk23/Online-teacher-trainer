Basic Shapes and Forms

Learn how to break down complex objects into simple shapes like circles, squares, and triangles. Understand the importance of shapes as the foundation for any drawing and practice converting everyday objects into these fundamental forms.

Shading Techniques

Explore the different ways to add depth and dimension to your drawings using shading. Learn about light sources, shadows, and how to create smooth transitions between light and dark areas. Practice with hatching, cross-hatching, stippling, and blending techniques.

Perspective Drawing

Master the basics of one-point and two-point perspective to create realistic depth in your artwork. Learn how to use a horizon line, vanishing points, and converging lines to give your drawings a 3D effect.
